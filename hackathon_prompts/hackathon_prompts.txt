We will have 4 hackathon prompts:

1. Cleveland Clinic, protein structure prediction

2. Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute - quantum key distribution (QKD)

3. Mila/Quandela/Autorite des Marches Financiers - finance/option pricing

4. Global Data Quantum/BasQ - quantum version of a Battleship/BombTester game

Or You can create your own project, on any topic, involving Quantum Computing, that you like

You can even focus on a new business, a startup, that interests you and describe:
1) the problem that the business solves
2) the customers who will be interested
3) how it relates to Quantum Computing 
4) how you will reach the customers
